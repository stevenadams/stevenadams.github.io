About

Hi, I'm Steven. A Front-End Developer out of the UK, with over 2 years experience building user interfaces for PHP, Python, Ruby, and C# projects. Focusing on scalability, speed, and user experience. Fascinated by new technologies and methodologies such as responsive design, task runners, and prototyping. I love working with agencies and helping to improve their front-end workflow. My everyday toolset includes Sass, JavaScript, HTML, Gulp, Sketch App, and Photoshop. I have a First Class Honours Degree in Interactive Multimedia Design from the University of Ulster. When I get some free time I can usually be found at the gym, behind a drum kit, or just relaxing.
